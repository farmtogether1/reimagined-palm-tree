An Introduction

What is AnonPay?

AnonPay is a project on the Binance Smart Chain, built with the vision of enabling the Effective Earning of Crypto, and Frictionless Spending in the Real World.

At the core of its Ecosystem Ecosystem is a fully automated Crypto to Credit utility that holders can use to conveniently spend their crypto at merchants worldwide, alongside other components such as an anonymity mixer, swap, staking/farming.

AnonPay's presale was held on 29 May 2022 at 1700hrs UTC, and filled successfully within a mere 10 minutes. A decision was made to proceed with the launch shortly after due to security concerns.

Crypto-to-Credit Features

With AnonPay’s Crypto to Virtual Credit Card (VCC) feature, holders can conveniently use $APAY to fund a VCC and spend with merchants worldwide.

Hassle Free

Any holder of $APAY can conveniently sign up for a VCC through our DApp for a one-time fee of $10 in APAY. Once issued, the card can be funded and used immediately.

No KYC

Throughout the process of swapping for $APAY and signing up for/loading the VCC, only your wallet address is exposed. Also, there is no way to link the VCC number to your wallet.

Widely Accepted

With Mastercard and Visa networks, the VCC will be very widely accepted. Use it pay for gadgets on Amazon, hail a ride on Uber or even convert to cash via certain platforms!
