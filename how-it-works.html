Step 2

Sign Up for Credit Card

From 15 June 2022, holders with at least 500,000 APAY can conveniently sign up for a Virtual Credit Card (VCC) through the AnonPay DApp. There will be a one time issuance fee of US$10 payable in $APAY during the sign up. The VCC will be issued within minutes of sign up.

Fund VCC with $APAY

Through the AnonPay DApp, easily use your $APAY to fund the VCC. A 1% fee (in $APAY) will be collected during each funding to upkeep the platform. Take note that the funding amount is dependant on the dollar value of $APAY tokens at the time of funding.

Start Spending!

Head off to your favourite online merchant and start spending! The VCC can be used at pretty much any online merchant that accepts Mastercard and Visa. Remember that the VCC balance and transaction details can be viewed live at anytime on the AnonPay DApp.
